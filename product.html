FLOWERSHOP offers different types of flowers.

Roses: prices can range from $2 to $10 per stem.

Tulips: the price per stem can range from $1 to $5.

Sunflowers: price per stem typically ranges from $3 to $6.

Orchids: prices can vary significantly, starting from $10 for a small potted orchid plant.

FLOWERSHOP involves activities such as flower design, flower arranging and also free flower delivery.
